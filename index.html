AI Community Leads UK and IE


AI Community Leads UK and Ireland is a network of people who run meet up groups or similar events focused around AI technologies.
It exists to help event organisers to share resources and ask for advice when needed and is not for profit.

How to get involved

  1. Download and read though our Code of Conduct.
  2. Join the community
  3. Check out Information and Resources our community has compiled from previous events.
  4. Visit our AI Communities Slack. Note you'll need to have joined the community to get access to this.
